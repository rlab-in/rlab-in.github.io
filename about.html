More About Our Group.

We are dedicated to make learning different aspects of robotics easy and fun. Our main goal is to make robotics technology easily accessible so that more young minds aspire towards this field. We are developing our competence in the area of Humanoid robotics movement and control, Compliant soft robotics for safe human-robot interaction and navigation of mobile robots and drones.
