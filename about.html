More About Our Group.

We are dedicated to make learning different aspects of robotics easy and fun. Our main goal is to make robotics technology easily accessible so that more young minds aspire towards this field. We are developing our competence in the area of Humanoid robotics movement and control, Compliant soft robotics for safe human-robot interaction and navigation of mobile robots and drones. If you are interested in robotics and would like to pursue your research interest in this field but demotivated by the fact that access to the robots is expensive and limited to few world class laboratories and universities, then this is the place for you. Our effort is to provide a platform where you can learn about advances in robotic technology. You can be the part of research projects offered and supervised by the researchers around the world and even able to perform experiments on simulators and robots to suport your research.
