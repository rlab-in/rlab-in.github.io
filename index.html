LEARN

Fundamental knowledge of mathematics, kinematics, dynamics, control and programming is neccessary to conduct research in robotics. Here not only we provide vast resources for self learning but also offer courses by our robotic researhers. The courses will be deliverd via hangout in real time. Also, our faculty can provide one-to-one intraction to clarify the concepts and software development principles for robotics.


More Info

APPLY

We strongly believe that the best way of learning is by doing. So the concepts and algorithms learned in the courses will be implemented on various robotics platforms. The experiments will be carried out either in simulation and on the real robots via tele-presence to have an experience as close as hands-on experience with the robots.

Participate

One more step to be a professional roboticist. Participate in one of the projects offered here! These research projects are volunteerily proposed by PhD candidates and several other researchers from various laboratories and universities in their personal capacities. Please browse the projects and be the part of one or more projects that suit your research interest.
