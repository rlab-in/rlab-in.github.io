VISION

If you are interested in robotics or humanoid robotics in particular and would like to pursue your research interest in this field in future but demotivated by the fact that access to the robots is expensive and limited to few world class laboratories and universities, then this is the place for you. Our effort is to provide a platform where you can learn about advances in robotic technology. Together with us, you can learn algorithms, develop software, test your software on robotics simulator and even able to perform experiments on the robots to support your research.

How does it work?

To develop skills in robotics, the best way of learning is by doing. Here we offer several courses right from the basics to advance courses in robotics. One can sign up for the courses which is interactive live courses where it is always possible to directly interact, discuss and clarify during the course or later. The concepts and algorithms learned in the courses will be implemented on various robotics platforms either in simulation and on the real robots via tele-presence to have an experience as close as hands-on experience with the robots.

Participate

Here we also work collaboratively on different projects. These research projects are voluntarily proposed by PhD candidates and several other researchers from various laboratories and universities in their personal capacities. Please browse the projects and be the part of one or more projects that suit your research interest. Moreover, if you are a robotics professionals and willing to offer some courses and practical experiments to our member students, we would be glad to hear from you. Please drop an email at admin@r-lab.in.
