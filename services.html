Project Rooms

If you are a graduate level student or Phd candiate and looking for better research platform or projects for your thesis and for your own learning, we will offer you various projects. Browse the offered projects and join the project by sending request. You will have to meet some eligiblity criteria defined by the project owner. To enhance your chance to be selected for the particular project, please see the requirement and take up some courses offered by us. Here, you will get opportunity to meet researchers and work with them directly. It may enhance your future prospects to be accepted as Phd and other positions later.
